Ente Locker is a simple app to securely store your important documents.

If you've been looking for a privacy-friendly way to store sensitive documents like IDs, passports, certificates, and other files, you've come to the right place. With Ente Locker, they are stored end-to-end encrypted (e2ee). This means that only you can view them.

We have open-source apps across Android, iOS, web and desktop, and your documents will seamlessly sync between all of them in an end-to-end encrypted (e2ee) manner.

Ente Locker also makes it simple to share your collections with your loved ones. You can organize documents into collections and share them securely.

Your encrypted data is replicated to 3 different locations, including a fall-out shelter in Paris. We take posterity seriously and make it easy to ensure that your documents are always safe and accessible.

We are here to make the safest document storage app ever, come join our journey!
