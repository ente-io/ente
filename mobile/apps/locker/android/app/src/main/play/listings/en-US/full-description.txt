Ente Locker is a secure vault for your important documents, credentials, and notes.

Everything you store is end-to-end encrypted before it leaves your device, so only you can access it.

FEATURES

- Store IDs, financial records, recovery details, and other personal documents
- Save secure notes alongside your files
- Share selected items privately with trusted people
- Set up emergency access for trusted contacts
- Sync across your devices with end-to-end encryption

PRIVACY

Your data is encrypted with your keys before upload. Ente's architecture and source code have been audited by Cure53.

SUPPORT

If you need help, please write to support@ente.io and someone will get in touch with you.
