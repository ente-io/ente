# Jump to Date

Jump to Date helps you quickly navigate to photos from a specific day in your gallery. When viewing any photo, you can jump to that date in your gallery.

## How to use Jump to Date

**On mobile:**

**From Memories:**
- Tap on the date to jump to that day in your gallery

**From file info:**
- Open any photo in the viewer
- Tap on the date to jump to that day in your gallery

Ente will open your gallery and automatically scroll to show photos from the same day as the selected photo.
