# Comments and Reactions

Comments and reactions let you interact with photos in collaborative albums, making it easy to share thoughts, ask questions, and express appreciation for moments captured by your family and friends.

## What are comments and reactions?

**Comments** allow you to have conversations about specific photos in shared albums. Perfect for:

- Asking questions about a photo ("Where was this taken?")
- Sharing memories and stories ("Remember when...")
- Coordinating with collaborators ("Can someone add more photos from this day?")
- Providing context and details about moments

## Who can comment and react?

Comments and reactions are available in **collaborative albums** based on your permission level:

**Collaborators can:**

- Add comments to any photo in the album
- React to any photo with emojis
- View all comments and reactions
- Edit or delete their own comments
- Remove their own reactions

**Admins can:**

- Everything collaborators can do, plus:
- Manage comments from other participants (if needed)

**Album owners can:**

- Everything admins can do, plus:
- Full control over comments and reactions in the album

**Viewers cannot:**

- Add comments or reactions (view-only access)

## How to add comments

**On mobile:**

1. Open the shared album
2. Tap on a photo to view it full screen
3. Look for the comment icon at the bottom
4. Tap the comment icon
5. Type your comment
6. Tap Send

**On web:**

1. Open the shared album
2. Click on a photo to open the viewer
3. Click the comment icon in the toolbar
4. Type your comment in the comment panel
5. Press Enter or click Send

**On desktop:**

1. Open the shared album in the desktop app
2. Click on a photo to view it
3. Click the comment icon in the bottom toolbar
4. Enter your comment
5. Press Enter or click Send

## How to add reactions

**On mobile:**

1. Open a photo in a shared album
2. Long press the photo or tap the reaction button
3. Choose an emoji from the reaction picker
4. The reaction appears on the photo instantly

**On web:**

1. Open a photo in the album viewer
2. Click the reaction button or hover over the photo
3. Select an emoji from the quick reaction menu
4. Your reaction is added immediately

**On desktop:**

1. View a photo in a shared album
2. Click the reaction icon in the toolbar
3. Choose your emoji reaction
4. The reaction appears on the photo

## Managing your comments and reactions

### Editing comments

You can edit your own comments after posting:

1. Find your comment on the photo
2. Tap/click the three-dot menu next to your comment
3. Select "Edit"
4. Make your changes
5. Save the edited comment

### Deleting comments

Remove your own comments anytime:

1. Open the comment you want to delete
2. Tap/click the three-dot menu
3. Select "Delete"
4. Confirm deletion

### Removing reactions

Remove a reaction you added:

**On mobile:** Long press your reaction and tap "Remove"

**On web/desktop:** Click your reaction again to remove it

## Viewing comments and reactions

### See all comments on a photo

**On mobile:**

- Tap a photo to view it full screen
- Tap the comment icon to see all comments
- Scroll through the comment thread

**On web/desktop:**

- Click a photo to open the viewer
- Click the comment icon to open the comment panel
- The panel shows all comments in chronological order

### See who reacted

Reactions appear as small emoji badges on photos:

- **In grid view**: Reaction emojis show below the thumbnail
- **In full view**: Reactions appear near the bottom of the photo
- **Tap/click a reaction**: See who added that reaction

Multiple people can use the same reaction - the count shows how many people reacted with each emoji.

## Notifications

Stay updated on conversations in your shared albums:

**You receive notifications when:**

- Someone comments on a photo you uploaded
- Someone replies to your comment
- Someone reacts to your photo
- There's activity in albums where you're a collaborator or admin

**Managing notifications:**

Control notification preferences in `Settings > Notifications` to:

- Enable/disable comment notifications
- Enable/disable reaction notifications
- Choose notification delivery method (push, email)
- Set quiet hours for notifications

## Privacy and visibility

**Within shared albums:**

- Comments and reactions are visible to **all participants** of the album (including viewers)
- Only collaborators, admins, and owners can add new comments or reactions
- Viewers can see all comments and reactions but cannot add their own

**Outside shared albums:**

- Comments and reactions are **private** to the shared album
- They are not visible in public links or collect links
- They don't appear in your personal copy of the photo
- Only album participants can see them

## Comments and reactions in collect links

For collect links (public links where anyone can add photos), comments and reactions are currently **not available**. This feature is designed specifically for collaborative albums between Ente users.

If you want to enable interaction on photos collected via public links, consider:

1. Converting collected photos to a collaborative album
2. Inviting specific people as collaborators
3. Enabling comments and reactions for those trusted participants

## Platform availability

**Mobile apps (iOS and Android):**

- Full support for comments and reactions
- Add, edit, delete, and view all interactions
- Real-time updates

**Web and desktop:**

- Full support for viewing comments and reactions
- Add comments and reactions
- Edit and manage your own interactions

## Best practices

**For effective communication:**

- Keep comments constructive and friendly
- Use reactions for quick responses
- Tag specific moments with descriptive comments
- Ask questions to engage other participants

**For album organization:**

- Use comments to provide photo context
- Coordinate photo collection through comments
- React to acknowledge you've seen important photos
- Keep conversations focused on the photos

## Technical details

Comments and reactions are:

- **End-to-end encrypted** like all Ente data
- **Synced in real-time** across all devices
- **Stored securely** on Ente's servers
- **Backed up** with your album data

When you remove a comment:

- It's permanently deleted from the album
- Other participants cannot see it anymore
- The deletion syncs across all devices
