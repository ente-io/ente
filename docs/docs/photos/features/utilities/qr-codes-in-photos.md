# QR codes in photos

Ente Photos can detect QR codes inside photos and screenshots, so you do not need to leave the app or use a second device to scan them.

## Availability

- **Platforms**: iOS and Android mobile apps
- **Supported files**: Photos and screenshots in the standard viewer

## Scan a QR code from a photo

**On mobile:**

1. Open a photo or screenshot that contains a QR code.
2. Touch and hold on the QR code in the photo.
3. Ente detects the QR code and shows its contents.
4. Choose what you want to do next:
   - **Open link** for web links
   - **Copy** to copy the detected contents
   - **Share link** or share the detected text with another app
   - **Pay** for supported UPI payment QR codes

If a photo contains more than one QR code, Ente can show each detected result separately.

## Tips

- QR code detection works nicely on screenshots, invitations, posters, and payment codes saved to your library.
- If nothing happens, zoom in a little and try long-pressing directly on the QR code area.
- For text-heavy screenshots, you can also use [Detect Text (OCR)](/photos/features/utilities/detect-text).

## Privacy

Detection happens on your device. Ente does not upload the detected QR contents just to scan them.
