# Ente Locker

Ente Locker is an end-to-end encrypted app for securely storing your important
documents and sensitive information. Store personal notes, account credentials,
physical record locations, and emergency contacts with the same security
guarantees as Ente Photos.

Locker is available for iOS and Android, with cross-device sync keeping your
information accessible wherever you need it.

## Key features

- **Four information types**: Store Personal Notes, Account Credentials,
  Physical Records, and Emergency Contacts
- **Collections**: Organize your documents into groups for easy access
- **End-to-end encryption**: Your data is encrypted before it leaves your
  device
- **Cross-device sync**: Access your information on all your mobile devices
- **Secure sharing**: Share with other Ente users or via public links
- **Lock screen protection**: Biometric or PIN authentication for app access
