# Emergency Contacts

Emergency Contacts let you store critical contact information for situations
when you need it most. Keep phone numbers, addresses, and notes for important
people in your life.

## Use cases

- Family members' contact details
- Doctor and medical specialists
- Lawyer and legal contacts
- Financial advisors
- Insurance agents
- Trusted neighbors
- Emergency services in different countries

## Creating an emergency contact

1. Open Ente Locker
2. Tap the **+** button
3. Select **Emergency Contacts**
4. Fill in the fields:
    - **Name**: Contact's name
    - **Contact Details**: Phone numbers, email, address, and relationship
      (freeform text field)
    - **Notes**: Additional information (optional)
5. Select a collection (optional)
6. Tap **Save**

## Features

### Flexible contact details

The Contact Details field is freeform, allowing you to store information in
whatever format works best:

```
Phone: +1 555-123-4567
Email: doctor@example.com
Office: 123 Medical Center Dr
Relationship: Primary care physician
```

### Important notes

Add relevant information in the notes field:

- "Speaks Spanish and English"
- "Available weekdays 9am-5pm"
- "For after-hours emergencies, call hospital main line"

## Organization tips

Create collections to organize your emergency contacts:

- **Medical**: Doctors, specialists, pharmacy
- **Family**: Close relatives with their roles
- **Professional**: Lawyer, accountant, insurance agent
- **Local**: Neighbors, local emergency services

## Sharing with family

Consider sharing your Emergency Contacts collection with trusted family
members so everyone has access to important contacts.

Learn more about [Sharing with users](/locker/features/sharing/share-with-users).
